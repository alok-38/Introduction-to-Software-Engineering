Welcome to the Thomas J. Watson Sr. Fan Club

Thomas J. Watson Sr. was a visionary leader and the founder of IBM, who played a key role in shaping the modern computing industry.

About Thomas J. Watson Sr.

Some Key Achievements

  • Founded IBM in 1911
  • Introduced the slogan "THINK"
  • Led IBM to become a global leader in computing
  • Contributed to the development of the computer industry

IBM Milestones

Year Milestone
1911 IBM was founded as Computing-Tabulating-Recording Co. (CTR)
1924 CTR was renamed IBM (International Business Machines)
1935 Introduction of the IBM 601 Accounting Machine
